Tim Berners-Lee

Tim Berners-Lee

Tim Berners-Lee(born June 8, 1955) is best known for inventing the World Wide Web. He implemented the first successful communication between a Hypertext Transfer Protocol(HTTP) client and server via the Internet in 1989. He is also the director of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C), which still effects the development of the Web. He is the founder of the World Wide Web Foundation.
