Bob Kahn

Robert Elliot "Bob" Kahn(born December 23, 1938) who along with Vint Cerf, invented the Transmission Control Protocol(TCP) and the Internet Protocol(IP) while working on a satellite packet network project. TCP and IP is fundamental communcication protocols at the heart of the Internet. Furthermorem Vint Cerf and Kahn co-founded the Internet Society, which was founded to provide leadership within Internet related standards, education, and policy.
