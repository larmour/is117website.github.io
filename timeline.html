Events that Shaped the Internet

  1. 1950: Licklider envisions idea of internet
  2. 1957: The Soviet Union launched Sputnik, the first artificial satellite
  3. 1958: Creation of ARPANET
  4. 1961: Packet switching created
  5. 1962: ARPANET launched
  6. 1971: Email is creation
  7. 1982: First public WAN initiated
  8. 1983: ARPANET transitions into TCP/IP
  9. 1989: Tim Berners-Lee invents World Wide Web
  10. 1990: Tim Berners-Lee creates HTTP and HTML
  11. 1995: Yahoo is a search engine
  12. 1958: President Eisenhower created Advanced Research Projects Agency(ARPA) to expand technology and science
  13. 1965:The world’s first electronic switching system in NJ
  14. 1969: The first Internet Messaging Processor(IMP) was delivered to Leonard Kleinrock
  15. 1973: First two networks in the world to use packet switching were connected together
  16. 1973: Vinton Cerf and Robert Kahn create TCP/IP protocol
  17. 1974: Vinton Cerf and Robert Kahm published “A Protocol for Packet Network Intercommunication ”
  18. 1974:The term “internet ” was coined by Vinton Cerf, Yogen Dalal and Carl Sunshine
  19. 1983: Paul Mockapetris invents domain name system
  20. 1988: Morris Worm was one of the first computer worms distributed through the internet
  21. 1989: Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web(WWW)
  22. 1991: WWW opens to the public
  23. 1991: Formation of Internet Society Announced
